Beef Stroganoff (for 4 people)

Ingredients:
- 1 pound beef sirloin, sliced into strips
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
- 1 cup beef broth
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook until softened. Add beef strips; cook until browned. Add mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes. Stir in flour; cook for 1 minute. Add beef broth; simmer until thickened. Stir in sour cream; heat through but do not boil. Season with salt and pepper.

Buttered Egg Noodles (for 6 people)

Ingredients:
- 12 ounces egg noodles
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Cook egg noodles according to package instructions. Drain and return to pot. Add butter and parsley; toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper.

Green Bean Almondine (for 2 people)

Ingredients:
- 1 pound green beans, trimmed
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 cup sliced almonds
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Blanch green beans in boiling water until tender-crisp; drain. In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add sliced almonds; cook until golden. Add minced garlic; cook for 1 minute. Add green beans; toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper.
