Thai Chicken Curry (for 4 people)

Ingredients:
- 1 pound chicken thighs, cut into pieces
- 2 tablespoons red curry paste
- 1 can (13.5 ounces) coconut milk
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup green beans, trimmed
- Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:
In a pot, cook red curry paste over medium heat until fragrant. Add chicken pieces; cook until browned. Stir in coconut milk, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Bring to a simmer. Add bell pepper and green beans; cook until vegetables are tender. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Coconut Rice (for 6 people)

Ingredients:
- 2 cups jasmine rice
- 1 can (13.5 ounces) coconut milk
- 1.5 cups water
- 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:
In a saucepan, combine jasmine rice, coconut milk, water, and salt. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 18 minutes or until rice is cooked.

Mango Salad (for 2 people)

Ingredients:
- 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
- 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 1 teaspoon fish sauce
- 1 teaspoon brown sugar

Instructions:
In a bowl, combine mango slices, red onion, and cilantro. In a separate bowl, mix lime juice, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Pour dressing over the mango mixture; toss to combine.
