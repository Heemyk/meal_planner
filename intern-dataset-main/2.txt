Spaghetti Carbonara (for 4 people)

Ingredients:
- 12 ounces spaghetti
- 4 ounces pancetta, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 large eggs
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. In a skillet, cook pancetta until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese. Drain spaghetti and return to pot. Quickly stir in the pancetta mixture and egg-cheese mixture, tossing to coat the pasta and cook the eggs with residual heat. Season with salt and pepper.

Caesar Salad (for 6 people)

Ingredients:
- 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1/2 cup croutons
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/3 cup Caesar dressing

Instructions:
In a large bowl, combine chopped romaine lettuce, croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese. Drizzle with Caesar dressing and toss to combine.

Garlic Bread (for 2 people)

Ingredients:
- 1/2 loaf French bread
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- Pinch of salt

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). In a small bowl, mix softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and salt. Slice the French bread and spread the garlic butter mixture on each slice. Wrap in foil and bake for 15 minutes.

Vegetable Lasagna (for 6 people)

Ingredients:
- 12 lasagna noodles
- 2 cups ricotta cheese
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups marinara sauce
- 2 cups spinach, chopped
- 1 zucchini, sliced
- 1 egg
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, spinach, salt, and pepper. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta mixture, zucchini slices, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top. Bake for 45 minutes.

Caprese Salad (for 2 people)

Ingredients:
- 2 large tomatoes, sliced
- 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Arrange tomato slices, mozzarella slices, and basil leaves on a platter, alternating them. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.

Baked Ziti (for 4 people)

Ingredients:
- 12 ounces ziti pasta
- 1 pound Italian sausage, casing removed
- 2 cups marinara sauce
- 1 cup ricotta cheese
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook ziti pasta according to package instructions. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook sausage until browned. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute. Stir in marinara sauce; simmer for 5 minutes. In a large bowl, combine cooked pasta, sausage mixture, and ricotta cheese. Transfer to a baking dish. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Bake for 25 minutes.

Garlic Knots (for 6 people)

Ingredients:
- 1 pound pizza dough
- 4 tablespoons butter, melted
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Divide pizza dough into small balls. Roll each into a rope and tie into a knot. Place on a baking sheet. In a bowl, mix melted butter, minced garlic, and parsley. Brush over knots. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake for 15 minutes or until golden.

Antipasto Salad (for 2 people)

Ingredients:
- 2 cups mixed greens
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/4 cup sliced black olives
- 1/4 cup diced salami
- 1/4 cup diced mozzarella cheese
- 2 tablespoons Italian dressing

Instructions:
In a bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, olives, salami, and mozzarella cheese. Drizzle with Italian dressing and toss to combine.
