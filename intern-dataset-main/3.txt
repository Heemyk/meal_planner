Beef Tacos (for 4 people)

Ingredients:
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 12 taco shells
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 cups shredded lettuce
- 1 tomato, diced
- Salsa and sour cream for serving

Instructions:
Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat. Add taco seasoning and water according to package instructions; simmer for 5 minutes. Fill taco shells with beef mixture, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheddar cheese. Serve with salsa and sour cream.

Chicken Quesadillas (for 2 people)

Ingredients:
- 2 large flour tortillas
- 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
- 1/4 cup diced onion
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions:
Heat half the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Place one tortilla in the skillet. Sprinkle half the cheese, chicken, bell pepper, and onion over the tortilla. Top with the second tortilla. Cook until the bottom is golden, then flip and cook the other side. Cut into wedges to serve.

Mexican Rice (for 6 people)

Ingredients:
- 1.5 cups long-grain rice
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
In a saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Add rice and cook until golden brown. Stir in onion and garlic; cook until softened. Add chicken broth, tomato sauce, cumin, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is tender.
